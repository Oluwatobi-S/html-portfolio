The Best Movies Of All-Time According to Tobi

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

The Black Panther

One of the most amazing storylines for an Action Superhero Movie. Not your average Superhero/Supervilan movie. I absolutely love Superhero movies.
Made me proud to be of African decent; more specifically Nigerian decent.

Blue Story

A movie that hits very close to HOME. A story that has shaped the rest of my life choices. I've watched it 3 times in a row LOL!

Gran Turismo

After seeing this movie, I felt unstoppable. Makes me want to continue to chase my dreams and not stop till it becomes my reality.
